The Green Scorpion Philosophy

At Green Scorpion was founded by Michelle Green, native Houstonian had a passion for marketing. After getting her second Business Bachelor Degree from the University of Houston, she worked for 15 years as a Director of Marketing while doing freelance work on the side, utilizing her second passion, Web Development. She hated seeing endless seas of reused code copied over and over, reproducing websites that all looked the same. She decided to do it different. No bloated templates or "plug-and-play" shortcuts.
