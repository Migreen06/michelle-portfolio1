About Us

How We Got Started

Green Scorpion was born in Houston, fueled by Michelle Green’s refusal to settle for a mediocre digital landscape. After double-majoring in Business at the University of Houston and grinding for 15 years as a Director of Marketing, Michelle spent her "free time" mastering the gut-level technicality of Web Development. She didn't just learn to code; she learned to weaponize it.

What We do

We’re here because we’re tired of the "seas of sameness"—the endless, bloated templates and lazy "plug-and-play" shortcuts that make every brand look like a carbon copy of its neighbor. At Green Scorpion, we color outside the lines because the lines are where businesses go to die. We are code-first artisans who believe your digital identity should be a lightning-fast, hand-coded masterpiece, not a drag-and-drop afterthought.



What We Don't Do

We don't do "safe." We build custom, high-performance engines for the B2B and DTC world that actually convert, because your brand deserves a backbone, not a template.

Our Mission
